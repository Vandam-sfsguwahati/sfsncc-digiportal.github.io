Aim of our Digital Portal


NCC | SFSN Company's Digital Portal aims at helping cadets of SFSN Company to stay connected over the internet and share their experiences and values imbibed while undergoing NCC Training. The Cadet's Corner has a lot more to explore ranging from archives of vidoes and photos and other services for our cadets.



Aim of NCC


To develop qualities of Character, Courage, Comradeship, Discipline, Leadership, Secular Outlook, Spirit of Adventure and Sportsmanship, besides the ideals of Selfless Service among the youth to make them useful citizens.



Pledge Of A Cadet


"We the cadets of National Cadet Corps, do solemly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizens of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings."
